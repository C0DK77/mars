Environmental uncertainty reduces precision and increases operator complexity.

Long-range precision systems face complex environmental variability—wind drift, atmospheric pressure, temperature gradients, platform instability. These factors introduce uncertainty. MARS transforms raw sensor data into actionable intelligence.
